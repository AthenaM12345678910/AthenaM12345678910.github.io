My Passion is Crocs

This Page is about crocs, the best cover for your feet to be invented by man! They even come in most styles, like the muppets.

Pros on Crocs:

  1. Crocs have two modes: leisure mode, when the strap is normal, and adventure mode, when the strap is behind the heel.
  2. Most are extremely stylish and pose no sort of fashion threat to you.
  3. Crocs are the most comfortable shoes that can be found or made.
  4. Crocs can easily be customized to your own liking.

Cons:

  1. Crocs can make your feet sweaty, but that's your problem. Don't try to blame the perfect Crocs for your bad case of Athlete's foot. Crusty.
  2. Some people customize their Crocs horribly. Don't be that person.
